Roasted Broccoli

Description

Roasted Broccoli is the perfect side dish for any protien. Its simple, easy, & quick not to mention how healthy it is

You can spice up this recipe with any of your prefered seasonings and even use frozen or fresh broccoli

Ingredients

  • 1 stalk of broccoli
  • 2 tbsps olive oil
  • 1 pinch of salt

Steps

  1. Chop broccoli into desireable sizes
  2. Coat broccoli with oil and salt
  3. Mix well
  4. Preheat oven to 425 degress F
  5. Roast broccoli for 25 min
